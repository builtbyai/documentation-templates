Our Story

Beauty in Every Petal

Born from a passion for natural beauty and botanical science, Bloom creates skincare that celebrates the power of flowers.

Our journey began in a small garden laboratory, where we experimented with rose water, lavender essence, and hibiscus extracts. What started as a personal quest for clean, effective skincare has blossomed into a beloved brand trusted by thousands.

Today, each product in the Bloom collection carries forward that spirit of botanical discovery. We work directly with sustainable farms around the world to source the finest ingredients, ensuring every bottle delivers the transformative power of nature.

Morning Ritual

Start Your Day Bright

A gentle morning routine that prepares your skin for the day ahead.

1

Cleanse

Start with our Gentle Flower Cleanser to remove overnight impurities without stripping your skin's natural oils.

2

Mist

Spritz Bloom Mist to refresh and prepare your skin for the next steps. The rose water balances pH levels.

Apply 3-4 drops of Rose Serum, pressing gently into skin. Allow to absorb for 30 seconds.

4

Finish with Petal Cream for all-day hydration and protection. Apply SPF over the top if heading outdoors.

FAQ

Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know about Bloom products and your skincare journey.

Are Bloom products suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes! All our products are dermatologist-tested and formulated without common irritants. We recommend patch testing new products and starting with our Gentle range if you have very reactive skin.

How long until I see results?

Most customers notice improved hydration immediately. For visible anti-aging benefits, we recommend consistent use for 4-8 weeks to see the full transformation.

Can I use Bloom products during pregnancy?

Most of our products are safe during pregnancy. However, we recommend consulting with your doctor first. Avoid products containing retinol alternatives during this time.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 60-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, return it for a full refund - no questions asked.

Are Bloom products vegan?

Yes, all Bloom products are 100% vegan and cruelty-free. We never test on animals and none of our ingredients are derived from animals.
