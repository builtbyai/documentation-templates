Letter from the CEO

2024 was a transformative year for Acme Corporation. Despite challenging market conditions, we delivered strong results and positioned ourselves for continued growth.

Dear Shareholders, I am pleased to report that Acme Corporation has delivered another year of solid performance, demonstrating the resilience of our business model and the dedication of our team. Revenue grew 18% year-over-year, reaching $4.2 billion, while adjusted EBITDA increased 22% to $890 million.

These results reflect our strategic focus on innovation, operational excellence, and customer centricity. We continued to invest in research and development, launching 12 new products that have been well-received by the market.

"Our purpose is clear: to build tomorrow's solutions that create lasting value for our customers, employees, and shareholders alike."

Sarah Chen Chief Executive Officer

Looking ahead, we see significant opportunities in emerging markets and new technology sectors. We are well-positioned to capitalize on these trends with our strong balance sheet, differentiated technology, and talented workforce of over 12,500 employees worldwide.

Sarah Chen

Chief Executive Officer

Strategic Priorities for 2025

As we enter 2025, our strategy remains focused on three key pillars that will drive sustainable long-term growth:

1

Innovation Leadership

Continuing to invest in R&D to maintain our technological edge. We plan to increase R&D spending by 15% in 2025, focusing on AI-powered solutions and sustainable technologies.

2

Global Expansion

Accelerating growth in high-potential markets across Asia-Pacific and Latin America, where we see significant demand for our solutions and strong economic fundamentals.

3

Operational Excellence

Driving efficiency across all operations through digital transformation, automation, and continuous improvement initiatives that enhance both margins and customer experience.

Our Vision for 2025

We remain committed to creating long-term value for our shareholders while making a positive impact on society. Our target is to achieve $5 billion in revenue while maintaining industry-leading profitability and advancing our ESG commitments.

Risk Management

Our enterprise risk management framework identifies, assesses, and mitigates risks across all aspects of our business.

HIGH

Cybersecurity Threats

Increasing sophistication of cyber attacks targeting enterprise systems. Mitigation: Enhanced security protocols, AI-powered threat detection, and employee training.

MEDIUM

Supply Chain Disruption

Ongoing supply chain volatility affecting component availability. Mitigation: Diversified supplier base, strategic inventory management, and nearshoring initiatives.

MEDIUM

Regulatory Compliance

Evolving regulations across jurisdictions, particularly in data privacy and AI. Mitigation: Proactive compliance programs and engagement with regulators.

LOW

Talent Competition

Competitive market for technology talent. Mitigation: Comprehensive compensation packages, culture initiatives, and career development programs.
