Philosophy

Rooted in Nature

Our commitment to sustainable fashion

At Botanica, we believe that fashion should exist in harmony with nature. Our designs are inspired by the organic forms, colors, and textures found in the natural world, translated into wearable art that celebrates both beauty and sustainability. Every decision we make is guided by our respect for the environment and the people who inhabit it.

Organic

100% organic certified materials

Ethical

Fair trade partnerships

Circular

Zero-waste design approach

Every piece in our collection tells a story of conscious creation. From seed to garment, we trace the journey of our materials, ensuring that each step honors both the people and planet involved in the process. We partner with family farms, artisan cooperatives, and sustainable mills to create fashion that feels as good as it looks.

In every walk with nature, one receives far more than they seek. Our garments are an invitation to carry that natural wisdom with you.

- Botanica Design Team

A Letter from Our Founder

Dear Friends,

When I founded Botanica seven years ago, I had a simple dream: to create beautiful clothing that doesn't cost the earth. Growing up on my grandmother's farm, surrounded by gardens and forests, I learned that nature provides everything we need if we treat it with respect.

The fashion industry had strayed far from these principles. Fast fashion, synthetic materials, exploitative labor practices - it all felt so disconnected from the natural rhythms I knew as a child. I wanted to create something different. Something that honored the earth and the hands that shape our garments.

This collection, Spring Awakening, is our most ambitious yet. We've partnered with organic cotton farmers in India, linen weavers in Belgium, and natural dyers in Japan. Every piece represents countless hours of craftsmanship and care. When you wear Botanica, you're not just wearing clothes - you're wearing a commitment to a better world.

Thank you for being part of this journey. Together, we're proving that fashion can be a force for good.

With gratitude,
Iris Chen
Founder & Creative Director

Sustainable Materials

Our commitment to sustainability begins with the materials we choose. Every fabric in the Botanica collection has been carefully selected for its environmental credentials, quality, and beauty.

Organic Cotton

GOTS-certified organic cotton from family farms in Gujarat, India. Grown without synthetic pesticides, using traditional farming methods that preserve soil health.

Belgian Linen

European Flax certified linen from Flanders. Linen requires minimal water and no irrigation, making it one of the most sustainable fibers available.

Peace Silk

Ahimsa silk that allows silkworms to complete their natural lifecycle. Sourced from small cooperatives in Karnataka who practice ethical sericulture.

Recycled Wool

Post-consumer wool fibers reclaimed and respun into new yarn. Each garment saves water and reduces textile waste in landfills.

Circular Fashion

We believe in fashion that lasts - and when it finally reaches the end of its life, we ensure it doesn't end up in a landfill. Our circular program gives every Botanica garment a second life.

Repair Program

Free repairs for life. Send us your worn Botanica pieces and our artisans will restore them to like-new condition. Buttons replaced, seams reinforced, love renewed.

Take Back Scheme

Return any Botanica garment for 20% off your next purchase. We'll repair and resell through our vintage collection, or responsibly recycle materials.

Compostable Packaging

All our packaging is 100% compostable. Tissue paper from recycled fibers, boxes from FSC-certified sources, and plant-based mailers that return to the earth.

Care

Care Guide

Proper care extends the life of your garments and maintains their beauty. Natural fibers require gentle handling but reward you with years of wear.

Organic Cotton

Machine wash cold, gentle cycle. Air dry when possible. Iron on medium heat. Natural dyes may soften over time - this is part of their character.

Belgian Linen

Machine wash warm. Tumble dry low or air dry. Embrace the natural wrinkles - they're part of linen's charm. Iron slightly damp for crisp finish if desired.

Peace Silk

Hand wash cold or dry clean. Lay flat to dry away from direct sunlight. Steam or iron on low heat. Store folded in breathable cotton bags.

Recycled Wool

Dry clean or hand wash cold with wool-specific detergent. Never wring. Lay flat to dry. Store with natural cedar to deter moths.
