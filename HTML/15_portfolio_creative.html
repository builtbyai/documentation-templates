Case Study

Solaris Energy

Brand identity for a renewable energy startup

Role Lead Designer
Timeline 3 months
Deliverables Identity, Guidelines, Website

Solaris Energy came to us with a mission: make solar energy accessible to every homeowner. They needed a brand that felt approachable and trustworthy, not cold and corporate like most energy companies.

We developed an identity system built around warmth and optimism, using a sunrise-inspired color palette and organic shapes that suggest growth and natural energy.
