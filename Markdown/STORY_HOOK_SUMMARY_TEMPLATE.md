        <li>Name tools: "PostgreSQL" not "database"</li>
        <li>Cite timeframes: "3.2 seconds" not "slow"</li>
        <li>Quote metrics: "$12K/month" not "expensive"</li>
      </ul>
    </div>

    <div style="background: rgba(255,255,255,0.15); backdrop-filter: blur(10px); padding: 25px; border-radius: 16px; border: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.2);">
      <div style="font-size: 2.5em; margin-bottom: 15px;">üé≠</div>

      <ul style="margin: 0; padding-left: 20px; line-height: 1.8; opacity: 0.95;">
        <li>Replace "it was hard" with specific obstacles</li>
        <li>Replace "I was stressed" with physical details</li>

        <li>Use dialogue and direct quotes when available</li>

      <ul style="margin: 0; padding-left: 20px; line-height: 1.8; opacity: 0.95;">
        <li>Front-load the most compelling information</li>
        <li>Cut unnecessary background details</li>
        <li>Vary sentence length for rhythm</li>
        <li>End paragraphs with forward momentum</li>
      </ul>
    </div>

    <div style="background: rgba(255,255,255,0.15); backdrop-filter: blur(10px); padding: 25px; border-radius: 16px; border: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.2);">
